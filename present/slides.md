## Reveal.js Tricks

Here are some built-in features you can use during the presentation:

- Press **ESC** or **O** to enter the slide overview.
- Press **S** to open the speaker view (shows notes & timer).
- Press **F** to enter fullscreen mode.
- Press **B** or **.** (period) to pause the presentation (black screen).
- Press **?** to show the help overlay with all keyboard shortcuts.

---

## Exporting to PDF

Reveal.js presentations can readily be exported to PDF:

1. Append `?print-pdf` to the end of the presentation URL.
2. Press **CTRL/CMD + P** to open the browser's print dialog.
3. Change the destination to **Save as PDF**.
4. Ensure margins are set to **None** and background graphics are **enabled**.
